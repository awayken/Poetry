The Bristling Ecstasy of Want

  1. The heart grows fat with More
  2. And keeps at bay the thoughts
  3. Of danger perched 'round doors
  4. And loss of all one's got'.
  5. As Comfort supplants Need
  6. And further supplants Want,
  7. To sleep, to drink, to feed
  8. Heap layers on the gaunt.
  9. While drunkenness will numb,
  10. So gluttony will slow.
  11. We deaf and blind and dumb
  12. Will stagnant excess know.
